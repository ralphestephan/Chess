Chess Details

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8x8 square board. Each player starts with 16 pieces: 1 king, 1 queen, 2 rooks, 2 knights, 2 bishops, and 8 pawns. The goal is to checkmate the opponent's king, meaning the king is in a position to be captured and cannot escape.
